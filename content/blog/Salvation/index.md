<h6 class="post-subtitle">Project Details</h6>
This Season Start cinematic was very nostalgic for me.  I grew up on stories of King Arthur and knights and shining armor.  In this piece we wanted to make it aspirational to be the virtuous hero and show the dream of a place dedicated to protecting those who are willing to protect eachother.  Demacia itself needed to be the overarching character that brought all the others together, a sanctuary in a world of magic.  Everything builds towards the arrival at the capital city where all the individual stories ideals' live.
